History

Chrysler, founded in 1925, became one of America's Big Three automakers and made a major contribution to World War II by mass producing tanks and war material. It was hit by financial difficulties following post-war prosperity, attempted a failed merger with Daimler, and was ultimately bailed out and merged with Fiat. Chrysler later merged with global auto giant Stellantis in 2021.
