History

Hyundai, originally founded as Hyundai Engineering and Construction Co. in 1947 by Chung Ju-yung, began as a construction firm. It rapidly expanded into various industries, including automotive and shipbuilding, eventually becoming South Korea's second-largest chaebol (conglomerate). Hyundai Motor Company, established in 1967, became the largest auto producer in South Korea.
