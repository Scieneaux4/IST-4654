History

Founded in 1948, Honda started out with motorized bikes and grew to become the world's largest motorcycle manufacturer. It went on to become famous globally for fuel-efficient cars like the Civic and launched the Acura luxury brand in the United States in 1986. Honda is currently a world leader in automobiles, motorcycles, and innovation, from hybrid technology to robotics.
